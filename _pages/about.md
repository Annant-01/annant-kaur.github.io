I am a PhD candidate studying pancreatic cancer, with a focus on tumor microenvironment dynamics and extracellular matrix remodeling.

My research investigates how tumor cells actively construct their own laminin-rich matrix, particularly Laminin-α5 (LAMA5), to support cancer cell survival and metastatic persistence.

I am especially interested in:
- Tumor-derived extracellular matrix and basement membrane biology  
- ECM-mediated signaling in cancer progression  
- Mechanisms of metastatic persistence and tumor cell survival within the microenvironment
